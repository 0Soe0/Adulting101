FAQ

Here you can find the most frequently asked questions and answers.

What is Adulting101?

Adulting101 is a platform where you can ask questions and get answers from other users, to make your life easier in any category, in every sense of the word.

How do I ask a question?

After creating an account you will find this option in various sections of our website, but the easiest way would be to do it from your profile page directly and clicking the "Ask a question" button.

How do I answer a question?

After creating an account you will find this option in every question's page, you can write comments, add pictures or videos to help the asker. We are also working in the posibility to make anonymous answers, but for now you will have to create an account to answer a question.

How do I upload a tutorial?

After creating an account you will find this option in various sections of the website, but the easiest and fastest way would be to do it from your profile page. You can upload a tutorial by clicking the "Upload a tutorial" button and following the instructions.
