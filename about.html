About us

We are a team of developers who are passionate about doing stuff ourselves, saving some money and also some stress! We are not only tired of freaking up, but we also want to help people who are going through the same thing, learning and growing from tiny adults to actual adults.

Our mission

No matter how old we are, we are never a "know it all". Our idea is to create a space where we all can learn from each other to make our lives easier in our everyday life. If you have a question, or want to share your knowledge, this is the place to do so. No matter the subject, how ridiculous or simple the task may sound, we listen and we don't judge. Let's grow together!
